# Rebuild With Hope

## Help Purnama Rebuild His Life Through Honest Work

Welcome to the official website project of **Rebuild With Hope**.

This website shares my personal journey of rebuilding my life after financial hardship. My goal is to leave interest-based debt behind, rebuild my livelihood through honest work, and provide transparent updates about my progress.

### Mission

- Build a halal and sustainable business.
- Share my journey openly and honestly.
- Provide transparent updates about donations and business progress.
- Create hope for a better future.

### Website Features

- Multi-language (Indonesia, English, Arabic)
- Responsive Design
- Donation Information
- Progress Updates
- Business Journey
- Contact Page
- Transparency & Documents

Thank you for visiting and supporting this journey.

May Allah reward every act of kindness.
